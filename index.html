LOOP Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

LOOP ("we", "our", or "us") is a golf round-logging app. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you sign up via Apple or Google.
  • Round data: Golf courses, scores, photos, and captions you log in the app.
  • Location: Used only when logging a round to auto-detect weather. We do not track you in the background.
  • Photos: Only photos you explicitly add to a round.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your rounds and feed within the app.
  • To provide weather data for rounds you log.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign In
  • Google Sign In
  • Google Places API (course search)
  • Open-Meteo (weather)
  • Supabase (database and storage)

Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

LOOP is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact

Questions? Email us at: hello@loopgolf.app (or your actual email)
